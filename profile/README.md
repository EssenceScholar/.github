# EssenceScholar

**AI-powered research intelligence for economists and business researchers.**

EssenceScholar connects your research profile to a library of AI agents that read, analyze, and expand academic literature on your behalf — so you spend less time processing papers and more time doing research.

## What we build

| Tool | What it does |
|------|-------------|
| **Paper Analysis** | Upload any PDF — AI agents extract key messages, methods, causal identification, novelty, and collaboration opportunities, all personalized to your research interests |
| **Deep Research** | Describe a research question; the system searches hundreds of papers and generates a structured literature review or research ideas report |
| **Submission Check** | Evaluates your manuscript against journal-specific criteria with a scored audit across structure, methodology, data, and academic standards |
| **Conference Optimizer** | Upload a conference agenda; AI ranks sessions and papers by relevance to your work |
| **Author Profiling** | Analyze co-authors and potential collaborators — h-index, FT50 publications, topic overlap with your research |
| **Research Podcasts** | Turn any paper into a personalized audio discussion |
